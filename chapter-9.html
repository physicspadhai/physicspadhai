Chapter 9: Ray Optics and Optical Instruments

               

1. Reflection at Plane Surface

       
            Laws: 1. $\angle i = \angle r$
            2. Incident ray, reflected ray, normal lie in same plane        
       

2. Reflection at Spherical Mirror

       
            Mirror Formula: $$\frac{1}{v} + \frac{1}{u} = \frac{1}{f}$$
            Magnification: $$m = \frac{h_i}{h_o} = -\frac{v}{u}$$
            Relation: $f = \frac{R}{2}$
            New Cartesian Sign Convention used        
       

3. Refraction at Plane Surface

       

Snell's Law:

       
            $$n_1\sin i = n_2\sin r$$ or $$\frac{\sin i}{\sin r} = \frac{n_2}{n_1} = \frac{v_1}{v_2} = \frac{\lambda_1}{\lambda_2}$$
            Refractive Index: $$n = \frac{c}{v}$$        
       

Apparent Depth:

       
            $$n = \frac{\text{Real Depth}}{\text{Apparent Depth}}$$
            Shift: $d = t\left(1 - \frac{1}{n}\right)$        
       

4. Total Internal Reflection (TIR)

       

Light traveling from denser to rarer, if $i > i_c$, no refraction occurs.

       
            Critical Angle: $$\sin i_c = \frac{n_r}{n_d} = \frac{1}{n}$$ for air
            Conditions: 1. Light denser → rarer 2. $i > i_c$        
       
            Applications: Mirage, Diamond sparkle, Optical fiber        
       

5. Refraction at Spherical Surface

       
            $$\frac{n_2}{v} - \frac{n_1}{u} = \frac{n_2 - n_1}{R}$$
            $n_1$ = medium of object, $n_2$ = medium of image        
       

6. Lens Maker's Formula

       
            $$\frac{1}{f} = (n - 1)\left(\frac{1}{R_1} - \frac{1}{R_2}\right)$$
            For lens in liquid: $$\frac{1}{f} = \left(\frac{n_l}{n_m} - 1\right)\left(\frac{1}{R_1} - \frac{1}{R_2}\right)$$        
       

Thin Lens Formula:

       
            $$\frac{1}{v} - \frac{1}{u} = \frac{1}{f}$$
            Magnification: $$m = \frac{h_i}{h_o} = \frac{v}{u}$$
            Power: $$P = \frac{1}{f}$$ Unit: Dioptre (D)        
       

Combination of Lenses:

       
            In contact: $$\frac{1}{F} = \frac{1}{f_1} + \frac{1}{f_2}$$
            $$P = P_1 + P_2$$, $$m = m_1 \times m_2$$        
       

7. Prism

       
            $$A + \delta = i + e$$, $$r_1 + r_2 = A$$
            At minimum deviation: $i = e$, $r_1 = r_2 = A/2$
            $$\delta_m = 2i - A$$
            Refractive Index: $$n = \frac{\sin[(A + \delta_m)/2]}{\sin(A/2)}$$        
       

8. Optical Instruments

       

Simple Microscope:

       
            $$M = 1 + \frac{D}{f}$$ for image at D = 25 cm
            $$M = \frac{D}{f}$$ for image at infinity        
       

Compound Microscope:

       
            $$M = m_o \times m_e = \frac{v_o}{u_o}\left(1 + \frac{D}{f_e}\right)$$
            Length: $L = v_o + u_e$        
       

Astronomical Telescope:

       
            Normal adjustment: $$M = -\frac{f_o}{f_e}$$, Length $L = f_o + f_e$
            Image at D: $$M = -\frac{f_o}{f_e}\left(1 + \frac{f_e}{D}\right)$$
